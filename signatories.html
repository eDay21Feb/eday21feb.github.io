e\(\rm -Day\) : 21st February

Signatories

Mathematicians and scholars supporting the designation of 21st February as \(e\)-Day

The following individuals have endorsed the proposal to designate 21st February as International \(e\)-Day. This list is updated as new signatories confirm their support.

Founding Signatories

  • Dr. Siegmund Probst Leibniz-Forschungsstelle (Leibniz Archive), Gottfried Wilhelm Leibniz Bibliothek, Hannover, Germany Co-author, "The Leibniz Catenary and Approximation of e" (Historia Mathematica, 2019)
